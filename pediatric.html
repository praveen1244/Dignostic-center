Comprehensive Pediatric Health Checkup

This health check is designed for children, covering a range of tests to assess and monitor various aspects of pediatric health.

Tests Included:

  • Physical Examination: Comprehensive examination to assess the child's overall health.
  • Developmental Milestones Assessment: Evaluates the child's physical and mental development.
  • Immunization Review: Ensures the child is up-to-date with recommended vaccinations.
  • Blood Tests: Includes complete blood count (CBC) and other relevant tests.
  • Urine Analysis: Checks for any abnormalities in the urine.
  • Vision and Hearing Screening: Assessments to detect any issues with vision or hearing.
  • Growth Chart Analysis: Monitors the child's growth patterns over time.
  • Nutritional Assessment: Evaluates the child's diet and nutritional status.
  • Common Pediatric Conditions Screening: Screens for conditions such as anemia, asthma, etc.
