Comprehensive Neurological Screening

This screening is designed to assess and monitor various aspects of neurological health.

Tests Included:

  • Neurological Examination: Comprehensive examination to assess neurological function.
  • Brain Imaging: MRI or CT scans to visualize the structure of the brain.
  • Electroencephalogram (EEG): Measures electrical activity in the brain to detect abnormalities.
  • Nerve Conduction Study: Evaluates how well the nerves transmit electrical signals.
  • Cognitive Function Assessment: Tests to assess memory, attention, and other cognitive functions.
  • Reflex Testing: Checks reflexes to assess the integrity of the nervous system.
  • Neuromuscular Imaging: Imaging studies to assess the health of nerves and muscles.
  • Evoked Potential Tests: Measures the electrical activity of the brain in response to stimuli.
  • Neurological Blood Tests: Checks for markers related to neurological health.
