Comprehensive Women's Wellness Package

This wellness package is designed specifically for women, covering a range of tests to assess and monitor various aspects of women's health.

Tests Included:

  • Pap Smear: Screens for cervical cancer and other abnormalities in the cervix.
  • Mammogram: X-ray examination of the breasts to detect breast cancer early.
  • Bone Density Test: Measures bone strength to assess the risk of osteoporosis.
  • Thyroid Function Tests: Checks thyroid hormone levels, which can impact women's health.
  • Hormone Level Check: Assesses levels of key hormones related to women's reproductive health.
  • Ovarian Cancer Blood Test: Screens for markers associated with ovarian cancer.
  • Cholesterol Level Test: Assesses cardiovascular health and risk of heart disease.
  • STD Testing: Screens for sexually transmitted infections for overall health.
  • Urinary Incontinence Evaluation: Assesses and addresses urinary health concerns.
