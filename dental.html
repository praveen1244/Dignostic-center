Comprehensive Dental Care Package

This care package is designed for maintaining and improving various aspects of dental health.

Services Included:

  • Dental Examination: Comprehensive examination to assess overall dental health.
  • Dental Cleaning (Prophylaxis): Professional cleaning to remove plaque and tartar.
  • X-ray Imaging: X-ray scans to visualize teeth and jaw structure.
  • Oral Cancer Screening: Checks for signs of oral cancer and abnormalities.
  • Fluoride Treatment: Application of fluoride to strengthen tooth enamel.
  • Sealants: Protective coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of molars.
  • Teeth Whitening: Cosmetic procedure to lighten teeth color.
  • Fillings and Restorations: Repairs cavities and restores damaged teeth.
  • Gum Disease Evaluation: Assessment of gum health and potential treatments.
