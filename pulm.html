Comprehensive Pulmonary Function Test (PFT)

This test is designed for assessing and monitoring various aspects of pulmonary health.

Tests Included:

  • Spirometry: Measures the amount and speed of air that is breathed in and out.
  • Lung Volume Test: Assesses the amount of air in the lungs and how well they expand.
  • Diffusion Capacity Test: Measures how gases move from the lungs to the blood.
  • Maximal Voluntary Ventilation (MVV): Assesses the maximum amount of air that can be breathed in and out in one minute.
  • Arterial Blood Gas (ABG) Test: Measures the levels of oxygen and carbon dioxide in the blood.
  • Peak Expiratory Flow (PEF): Measures how fast a person can exhale air.
  • Forced Expiratory Volume (FEV1): Measures the amount of air that can be forcibly exhaled in one second.
  • Pulmonary Function Questionnaire: Assesses symptoms and risk factors related to pulmonary health.
