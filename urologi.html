Comprehensive Urological Health Checkup

This health check is designed for assessing and monitoring various aspects of urological health.

Tests Included:

  • Urological Examination: Comprehensive examination to assess urological health.
  • Prostate Specific Antigen (PSA) Test: Measures PSA levels to screen for prostate issues.
  • Kidney Function Tests: Evaluates the health and function of the kidneys.
  • Urinalysis: Analyzes urine for signs of kidney disease or other conditions.
  • Bladder Ultrasound: Uses ultrasound to assess the health of the bladder.
  • Urodynamic Testing: Measures the function of the urinary system.
  • Cystoscopy: Examines the inside of the bladder and urethra.
  • Testicular Ultrasound: Uses ultrasound to assess the health of the testicles.
  • Urological Blood Tests: Checks for markers related to urological health.
