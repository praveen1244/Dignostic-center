Comprehensive Dermatological Screening for Skin Health

This health check includes a comprehensive dermatological screening for skin health. It covers a range of tests to assess and monitor the condition of your skin.

Tests Included:

  • Skin Examination: Visual inspection of the skin to identify any abnormalities or skin conditions.
  • Mole Mapping: Mapping and monitoring of moles to detect any changes that may indicate skin cancer.
  • Biopsy: Removal of a small sample of skin for laboratory analysis to diagnose skin conditions.
  • Allergy Testing: Identifies allergens that may contribute to skin issues.
  • Microscopic Examination: Examines skin cells under a microscope for detailed analysis.
  • Phototherapy: Treatment using ultraviolet light to address certain skin conditions.
  • Patch Testing: Identifies contact allergens that may cause skin irritation.
  • Sebum Analysis: Measures the amount of sebum (skin oil) to assess skin health.
  • Microbiome Analysis: Analyzes the microbiota on the skin to understand its impact on skin health.
  • Wound Culture: Collects and analyzes samples from skin wounds to identify infections.
  • Elasticity Test: Measures the skin's elasticity to assess signs of aging and dehydration.
  • UV Damage Assessment: Evaluates skin damage caused by ultraviolet (UV) radiation.
