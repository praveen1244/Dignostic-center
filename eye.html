Comprehensive Ophthalmic Health Checkup

This health check is designed for assessing and monitoring various aspects of eye health.

Tests Included:

  • Eye Examination: Comprehensive examination to assess overall eye health.
  • Visual Acuity Test: Measures the sharpness of vision.
  • Retinal Imaging: Captures images of the retina for detailed examination.
  • Intraocular Pressure (IOP) Test: Measures pressure within the eye to screen for glaucoma.
  • Visual Field Test: Evaluates the full horizontal and vertical range of vision.
  • Color Vision Test: Assesses the ability to perceive different colors.
  • Corneal Topography: Maps the surface of the cornea for abnormalities.
  • Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT): Provides detailed cross-sectional images of the retina.
  • Eye Health Questionnaire: Assesses symptoms and risk factors related to eye health.
