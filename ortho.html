Comprehensive Orthopedic Health Checkup

This health check is designed for assessing and monitoring various aspects of orthopedic health.

Tests Included:

  • Orthopedic Examination: Comprehensive examination to assess musculoskeletal health.
  • X-ray Imaging: X-ray scans to visualize bones and joints.
  • Bone Density Scan: Measures bone density to assess the risk of osteoporosis.
  • Joint Function Assessment: Evaluates the range of motion and function of joints.
  • Muscle Strength Testing: Assesses the strength and functionality of muscles.
  • Orthopedic Blood Tests: Checks for markers related to musculoskeletal health.
  • Arthritis Screening: Screens for signs of arthritis and related conditions.
  • Spinal Health Assessment: Evaluates the health of the spine and vertebral discs.
  • Orthopedic Ultrasound: Uses ultrasound to assess soft tissues around joints.
