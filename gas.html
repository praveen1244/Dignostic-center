Comprehensive Gastrointestinal Health Checkup

This health check is designed for assessing and monitoring various aspects of gastrointestinal health.

Tests Included:

  • Colonoscopy: Examines the colon for abnormalities and signs of colorectal cancer.
  • Upper Endoscopy: Investigates the upper gastrointestinal tract for conditions such as ulcers and inflammation.
  • Stool Test: Analyzes stool samples for signs of gastrointestinal bleeding or infections.
  • Liver Function Tests: Evaluates the health and function of the liver.
  • Abdominal Ultrasound: Uses ultrasound to assess organs in the abdominal cavity.
  • Esophageal Manometry: Measures muscle contractions in the esophagus.
  • CT Enterography: Provides detailed images of the small intestine using CT scans.
  • Gastrointestinal Blood Tests: Checks for markers related to gastrointestinal health.
  • Gastrointestinal Health Questionnaire: Assesses symptoms and risk factors related to gastrointestinal health.
